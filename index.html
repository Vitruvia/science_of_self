Welcome to The Science Of Self.

This website has the goal of discussing technologies that allow individuals to have greater agency over their lives. It also delivers the fundamentals required to understand these techonologies and the way of life that underlies their use.

The topics of the website can be separated as follows:

Topic Description
Fundamentals Discussions about the mindset behind the Science of Self. Also includes posts about how to enable the spreading of this mindset and the development of the technologies to concretize it. Areas of knowledge involved here are politics, education, philosophy, personal opinions, as well as the basic sciences necessary to understand and create the technologies endorsed by the Science of Self.
Existing Technologies Technologies for self-improvement that already exist. All "Technologies" listed here involve both cutting edge novelties such as the latest laser therapy for hair removal and the most basic methods such as meditation and dieting.
Emerging Technologies This topic includes technologies that are under development or that could enter development in the imediate future, considering current knowledge of the basic sciences and the state of existing technologies.
Emerging Technologies This topic includes technologies that are under development or that could enter development in the imediate future, considering current knowledge of the basic sciences and the state of existing technologies.
Speculative Technologies Speculative Technologies are those that could be developed in an unknown future, and that usually depend on the development of other technologies and on the advancement of basic science knowledge to a point that humanity has not yet achieved. Some speculative technologies might not even be provable possible, but they should be at least considered to have a chance of being developed to be included here.
